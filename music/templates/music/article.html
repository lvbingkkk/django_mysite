Samadhi Maya the illusion of the self



    Samadhi is an ancient Sanskrit word for which there's no modern equivalent. There's a fundamental challenge with making a film about Samadhi. Samadhi points to something that can't be conveyed on the level of mind. This film is simply the outer manifestation of my own inner journey. The intention is not to teach you about Samadhi or provide information for your mind, but to inspire you to directly discover your true nature.
      Samadhi是一个古老的梵语单词，没有现代对应的词。制作一部关于三摩地的电影是一个根本性的挑战。三摩地指出了一些在头脑层面上无法传达的东西。这部电影只是我自己内心旅程的外在表现。意图不是教你关于三摩地的知识或为你的思想提供信息，而是激励你直接发现你的真实本性。

    Samadhi is relevant now more than ever. We are at a time in history where we have not only forgotten Samadhi, but we've forgotten what we forgot. This forgetting is Maya, the illusion of the self. As humans, ost of us live immersed in our daily lives with little thought of who we are , why we are here, or where we're going. Most of us have never realized the true self, the soul, or what the Buddha called anata, that which is beyond name and form. beyond thinking. As a result , we believe we ar these limited bodies. We live in fear, either conscious or unconscious, that the limited self structure that we are identified with will die. In today's world, the vast majority of people who are engaged in religious or spiritual practices, such as yoga, prayer, meditation, chanting, or any kind of ritual, or practicing techniques which are conditioned which means they are just part of the ego construct. The seeking and the activity isn't the problem. Thinking you have found the answer in some external form is the problem. Spirituality, in its most common form, is on different than the pathological thinking that is going on everywhere. It is a further agitation of the mind. More human doing as opposed to human being.
      三摩地现在比以往任何时候都更有意义。我们正处于一个历史时期，我们不仅忘记了三摩地，而且忘记了我们忘记的东西。这种遗忘是玛雅，自我的幻觉。作为人类，我们沉浸在日常生活中，很少考虑我们是谁，我们为什么在这里，或者我们要去哪里。我们大多数人从未意识到真正的自我，灵魂，或佛陀所说的anata，这超越了名字和形式。超越了思考。因此，我们认为我们是这些有限的机构。我们生活在有意识或无意识的恐惧中，即我们认同的有限自我结构会死亡。在当今世界，绝大多数人从事宗教或精神实践，如瑜伽、祈祷、冥想、念诵或任何类型的仪式，或练习有条件的技巧，这意味着他们只是自我结构的一部分。寻求和活动不是问题。认为你已经以某种外部形式找到了答案，这就是问题所在。灵性，以其最常见的形式，与到处都在发生的病态思维不同。这是心灵的进一步激动。更多的人类做事，而不是人。

   The ego construct wants more money, more power, more love, more of everything. Those on the so-called spiritual path desire to be more spiritual, more awake, more equanimous, more peaceful, more enlightened. The danger for you watching this film is that your mind will want to acquire Samadhi. Even more dangerous is that your mind might think it has acquired Samadhi. Whenever there's a desire to attain something, you can be sure it's the ego construct at work. Samadhi is not about attaining or adding something more to yourself. To realize Samadhi is to learn to die before you die. Life and death are like yin and yang, an inseparable continuum endlessly unfolding with no beginning and no end. When we push away death, we also push away life. When you experience the truth directly of who you are, there is no longer any fear of life or death.
      自我构造需要更多的钱，更多的权力，更多的爱，更多的一切。那些在所谓的精神道路上的人渴望更有灵性，更清醒，更平静，更和平，更开明。你看这部电影的危险在于，你的头脑会想要获得三摩地。更危险的是，你的头脑可能会认为它已经获得了三摩地。每当有获得某样东西的欲望时，你可以确定这是工作中的自我构造。Samadhi不是要获得或给自己增加更多的东西。意识到三摩地就是学会在死前死去。生与死就像阴阳，一个不可分割的连续体无休止地展开，没有开始也没有结束。当我们推开死亡时，我们也推开生命。当你直接体验到自己是谁的真相时，就不再有生或死的恐惧。
